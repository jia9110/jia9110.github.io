土木工程系海外学者短期讲学课程：高级交通网络分析——交通网络与大数据分析
 
【课程名称】高级交通网络分析——交通网络与大数据分析（Advanced Transportation Network Analysis—Networks and Big Data for Smart Transportation Analytics）
【课程编号】Y0030191
 
【授课对象】研究生及高年级本科生
 
【授课语言】英文
 
【上课时间】2017年7月10日 8:00-11:30；11日：8:00-11:30；7月13日 8:00-11:30；14日：8:00-11:30
 
【上课地点】新土木馆（何善衡楼）300教室
 
【考核方式】考查，1学分
 
【报名方式】手动选课，即日起接受报名。请准备选课的同学在2017年7月9日之前将“学号”“姓名”“所在院系”“联系方式（手机）”信息发送至 604612083@qq.com 报名，邮件请注明“选课申请”。
 
【课程简介】
 
本课程将侧重于网络与数据挖掘在交通运输系统中的运用。主要会有关于基础网络分析、统计分析、数据清理及基于大数据的数据挖掘技术等相关主题的讲解与应用。学生将学会使用所教授的概念和方法去解决他们感兴趣的研究领域。
 
本课程适于土木工程系、自动化系、汽车工程系、工业工程系相关专业研究生来选。
 
【教学大纲】
  1. Introduction: [lecture 1] What is data mining? What makes it a new and unique discipline? Relationship between Data Warehousing, On-line Analytical Processing, and Data Mining.
[lecture 2&3]  Preliminaries of Network Analysis and Optimization.
[lecture 4] Data Warehousing and Data mining process: Data preparation/cleansing, task identification.
  1. Association rule: [lecture 5] Association Rule Mining: Apriori and FP growth.
  2. Classification: [lecture 6 and 7] Bayes classifier
  3. Clustering :  [lecture 8] basics of clustering and mixture models.
  4. Networks : [Lecture 9 and 10] Notion of Equilibrium and how to model taxi markets using big data and network analysis
  5. Network Structure : [Lecture 11 and 12] How to measure large scale networks, structure and function and their applications to smart mobility
  6. Classification techniques in networks with big data
[lecture 13] k-means and k-medoids.
[lecture 14] supervised learning – rule-based classification.  
  1. Summary of recent advances in networks and big data in transportation [lecture 15] Instructor will discuss various challenges and experience with big data analytics and self driving cars research if time permits
  2. Presentation by students related to their research [lecture 16]
Note: Some of these topics may be covered early or late depending on the questions and pace in the class.
A student completing this course is expected to be able to:
  1. Prepare and clean datasets for analysis of patterns
  2. Apply the concept of regression using datasets
  3. Understand basic concepts of game theory in transportation systems
  4. Apply the concept of clustering to identify trends within datasets
  5. Be aware of the concepts of supervised learning algorithms to analyze data
  6. Apply the concepts to emerging smart mobility applications such as taxi analytics, smart transportation
 
主要教材：无指定教材，参考课堂讲义
 
【授课教师简介】
 
Ukkusuri教授普度大学教授，是交通运输规划与管理领域国际著名学者，在交通规划、交通网络分析方面研究深入，在交通大数据和数据驱动的交通模型研究方面取得了众多学术成果。Ukkusuri教授现今总共发表了超过180篇期刊论文，其在交通领域顶级期刊论文数及引用次数均处于同行前列，学术贡献突出。Ukkusuri教授同时兼任多个交通领域期刊编委职务，包括Transportation Research Part B, C, Network Spatial Economics, Transportmetrica Part B。
 
Ukkusuri教授在其任职的Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute以及Purdue University开设过总共12门课程，包括交通系统建模，动态交通分配，交通工程，交通控制以及交通货运系统等课程。
