各位同学：
    大家好！  
    邱勇校长11月份开放交流时间定于 11月30日（周四）10:00-12:00 ，欢迎同学们积极参加，请将“姓名、院系、年级、手机号码、希望交流的内容”发送至报名邮箱 zzbs@tsinghua.edu.cn ，地点将另行通知。

Qiu Yong’s Open Office Hour
Dear all students,
    The November session Open Office Hour of Qiu Yong , President of Tsinghua University, will be held on Thursday 10:00-12:00, Nov 30 . Students who expect to in-person meeting please submit your name, school and department, phone number and issues of concern to zzbs@tsinghua.edu.cn , the location will be informed later.
    All students are welcome to participate.
 
Registrar’s Office
Nov 28,2017
