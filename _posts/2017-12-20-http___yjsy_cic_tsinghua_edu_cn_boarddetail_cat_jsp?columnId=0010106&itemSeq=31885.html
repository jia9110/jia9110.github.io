2018 AFLSP Scholarship Applications 2018年“亚洲未来领袖奖学金计划”开放申请

  • 发布时间:2017-12-20    访问计数:1137
  • [关闭窗口]

根据百贤亚洲研究院的通知， 2018 年 “ 亚洲未来领袖奖学金计划 ” 申请现已开放，申请开放时间为 2017 年 12 月 1 日至 2018 年 1 月 31 日。

我校作为该奖学金计划的参与院校，申请报读或现正就读我校全日制学位生课程的亚洲学生（仅限来自日本，韩国，中国内地、香港特别行政区、澳门特别行政区、台湾）可申请“亚洲未来领袖奖学金”。

具体申请信息详见百贤亚洲研究院官网，网址： https://www.bxai.org/zh-cn/

申请资格和甄选准则： https://www.bxai.org/zh-cn/programs-and-scholarships/scholarships/selection-criteria

申请程序与时间： https://www.bxai.org/zh-cn/programs-and-scholarships/scholarships/application-process-and-timeline

申请端口： https://www.bxai.org/zh-cn/applicants/login

 

    Applications for the Asian Future Leaders Scholarship Program (AFLSP) for Participating Universities (PUs) are now open from 1 December 2017 to 31 January 2018.

   Tsinghua University is one of the participating universities of AFLSP. Asian students from Japan, Korea and Mainland China, Hong Kong SAR, Macao SAR, Taiwan applying for a full-time degree program or who are currently enrolled at Tsinghua University are eligible for the AFLSP.

   For more information about AFLSP application, please visit the website of Bai Xian Asia Institute at https://www.bxai.org/zh-cn/

   For Eligibility and Selection Criteria, please visit： https://www.bxai.org/zh-cn/programs-and-scholarships/scholarships/selection-criteria

   For Application Process & Timeline, please visit: https://www.bxai.org/zh-cn/programs-and-scholarships/scholarships/application-process-and-timeline

   To apply, please visit: https://www.bxai.org/zh-cn/applicants/login
