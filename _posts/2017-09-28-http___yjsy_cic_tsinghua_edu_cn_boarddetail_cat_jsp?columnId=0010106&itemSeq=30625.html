【课程简介】
 
This course will introduce a number of econometric models that are important to transportation data analysis. The application of such models ranges from traffic safety analysis to travel demand modeling and policy evaluation. Students will learn to specify the models based on data characteristics and model properties. They will also learn to estimate these models using appropriate techniques.  Equally importantly is that students will learn to interpret the analysis results in a rigorous and meaningful way. This course provides an opportunity to develop the skills of studying transportation issues through statistical data analysis and to gain hands-on experience with statistical analysis software. The course will emphasize application, but the underlying theory and limitations will also be discussed to ensure that the methods are fully understood, properly applied and potentially enhanced.
Prerequisites for the course include basic linear algebra, matrix analysis and probability and statistics. Therefore, students will be expected to understand the basic concepts of statistical distributions, hypothesis testing, graphical methods, matrix manipulation, etc. upon entering the course.
 
本课程适于土木工程系、自动化系、汽车工程系、工业工程系相关专业研究生来选。
