TA Needed     [外文系业务办   2018-01-08]

English sessions (60640012/90640012) for Non-English major graduate students need some teaching assistants (both Chinese and overseas). The applicants must be full-time graduate students of Tsinghua University and must meet the following requirements.

  1. Meet the related policies in Regulations for Teaching Assistant, Research Assistant and Administration Assistant, Tsinghua University.
  2. Have excellent communicative skills in English; international TA’s mother tongue must be English;
  3. Be passionate about whatever tasks assigned by the professor;
  4. Answer students’ questions with patience;
  5. Be able to work 12~15 hours per week. Be punctual all the time;

PAY:

Teaching Assistants will be paid around 10th each month and 5 times each semester. Doctoral students get 2600 rmb per month and master students 1600 rmb per month.

Students who are interested may send your application form with any proof to hongli-wang@mail.tsinghua.edu.cn under the title of “your name + application” by 5 pm Jan. 16, 2018. You will receive an email for the interview if you win the first round competition.

 

Department of Foreign Languages and Literatures
