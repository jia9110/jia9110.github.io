微纳电子系海外学者短期讲学课程--模拟集成电路设计前沿与挑战
 
海外学者短期讲学课程报名通知
 
【课程名称】：模拟集成电路设计前沿与挑战
New Frontiers and Challenges of Analog Integrated Circuits Design
【课程号】：Y0260081
【学时】：16学时
【授课语言】：中文
【上课日期】：2017.07.03~2017.07.07（周一到周五）
【上课时间】：下午2:00~5:00 (3h)
【上课地点】：微电子所新所312会议室
【考核方式】：考查
【授课对象】：研究生
【课容量】：20人
【主讲教师】：孙楠教授  （University of Texas at Austin, USA）
 
【个人简历】：
Nan Sun is Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin. He received the B.S. degree from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China in 2006, where he ranked top in Department of Electronic Engineering. He received the Ph.D. degree from the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences at Harvard University in 2010.
 
Dr. Sun is a Fellow of the AMD Chair in Computer Engineering at UT Austin. He received NSF Career Award in 2013 and Jack Kilby Research Award from UT Austin in 2015. He also received Samsung Fellowship, Hewlett Packard Fellowship, and Analog Devices Outstanding Student Designer Award in 2003, 2006, and 2007, respectively. He won Harvard Teaching Award in three consecutive years: 2008, 2009, and 2010. He serves in the technical program committee of IEEE Custom Integrated Circuits Conference and Asian Solid-State Circuit Conference . He is Associate Editor for IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems – I, Regular Papers .
 
His research interests include: 1) analog, mixed-signal, and RF integrated circuits; 2) miniature spin resonance systems; 3) magnetic sensors and image sensors; 4) developing micro- and nano-scale solid-state platforms (silicon ICs and beyond) to analyze biological systems for biotechnology and medicine.
 
【预备知识】：模拟电路设计，数字信号处理，数模转换器设计
 
【课程简介】：这门暑期课程将介绍模拟集成电路方向最前沿的研究进展。内容包括各种类型高性能的数模/模数转换器，超低功耗的放大器/滤波器，基于时间域和相位域的模拟信号处理，模拟运算和模拟神经网络，以及压缩识别技术。在课程进行当中，我们将阅读很多最近发表的文献，我们也将讨论到目前为止还没有解决的关键技术问题。这门课程的目标不是对新技术的简单介绍，而是要培养直觉理解并且启发创新。这门课程欢迎对模拟集成电路设计有热情并愿意钻研的学生。
 
【课程大纲】：
1.       Advanced SAR ADC
1.1   Low-power SAR ADC
1.2   High-speed SAR ADC
1.3   Time-interleaved SAR ADC
1.4   High-resolution noise shaping SAR ADC
2.       Advanced Delta-sigma ADC and DAC
2.1 Mismatch shaping techniques
2.2 Wide-band continuous-time delta-sigma ADC
2.3 Loop filter design with conditionally stable amplifier
3.       Time/phase-domain analog signal processing techniques
3.1 VCO based open-loop ADC
3.2 VCO based closed-loop ADC
3.3 VCO based capacitance-to-digital converter
4.       Ultra-low-power sensor interface design
4.1 Ultra-low-power amplifier
4.2 Ultra-low-power reference
5.       Compressive sensing techniques
5.1 Compressive-sensing theory
5.2 Compressive-sensing based single-channel ADC
5.3 Compressive-sensing based multi-channel ADC
6.       Other advanced topics
6.1 Analog neural networks
6.2 Analog design automation
