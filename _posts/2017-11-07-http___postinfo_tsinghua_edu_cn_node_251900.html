Global Culture Workshop

 

想改善英语口语和听力、也对其他国家的文化有兴趣的同学，请注意一下！

 

清华的外文系已经开始开Global Culture Workshop。所有的清华学生欢迎来参加！本硕博不限，只要你是清华的学生就可以扫一下二维码加入。如果你已经加入我们的某一个Global Culture Workshop 微信群，就不用扫了。

 

这周二会有来自美国、俄罗斯、澳大利亚和巴基斯坦的4位外国老师给大家介绍各自国家有关经济的信息。这门课是英语角和讲座的结合。每周二和周五晚上7点到9点在文南楼116教室。记得扫一下二维码了解更多！

 

Date: Weeks 2-16, starting September 26

 

Time: Tuesdays and Fridays, 7-9 pm

 

Objective: To broaden the horizons of Tsinghua students by introducing them to different countries and regions of the world and to give all Tsinghua students the opportunity to improve their English skills.

 

Location: Room 116, Wennan Building

 

Organised by: Tsinghua University Department of Foreign Languages and Literatures

 

How to register: This course is open to all Tsinghua students. Students wanting to attend the class should scan the attached QR code.

 

 

Course content:

Join this course to learn about the countries of our lecturers – Ghana, Denmark, Australia, Pakistan, the United States and Russia. This course is a series of workshops that will cover topics like education, politics and film. The workshops will be instructed in part-lecture, part-interactive style. Students will have the chance to practise their English speaking and listening skills during the class.

 

Examples of some topics that may be covered are:

Is polygamy really that common in Pakistan?

Why is Denmark one of the happiest places to live?

What does the music of Ghana sound like?

Which scholarships can help you study in Australia?

What are the obstacles to gun control in the US?

How do Russian superstitions compare to those in China?
